# where (to)

*To* is often dropped after *where*.

- ***Where** are you going **(to)**?*
- ***Where** does this road lead **(to)**?*

*To* is not normally dropped in the short question *Where to?*

- *‘Could you send this off for me?’ ‘**Where to?’***
