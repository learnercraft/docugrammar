# lose and loose

*Lose* (pronounced /luːz/) is an irregular verb (*lose* – *lost* – *lost*).

*Loose* (pronounced /luːs/) is an adjective (the opposite of *tight*).

- *I must be **losing** weight – my clothes all feel **loose** .

(not *~~I must be loosing weight …~~*)
